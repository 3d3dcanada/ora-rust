# The $10M ARR Playbook: Human-Centric GTM Strategy

Building a $10M+ SaaS product requires moving away from automation-heavy spam and moving toward deep, value-driven human interactions. This is the Go-To-Market strategy for ORA.

## 1. Documentation as the Primary Marketing Engine
In 2026, developers do not read landing pages; they read Docs. 
*   **Strategy:** Our documentation must be treated as our flagship marketing asset. Every feature must have a working, copy-pasteable Rust/TypeScript integration example.
*   **Human Touch:** Include "Why we built it this way" boxes authored by actual engineers, discussing the trade-offs we made (e.g., choosing Qdrant over PgVector for latency). Vulnerability builds trust.

## 2. Community & Peer-to-Peer Growth (Discord & GitHub)
Traditional B2B marketing funnels are dead for developers. Growth happens in Slack and Discord.
*   **Strategy:** Launch the "ORA High Command" Discord server. Not for marketing, but for architects solving hard multi-agent security problems. 
*   **Human Touch:** The founders/lead devs must be in the trenches answering questions daily. If a user hits a bug, we patch it natively in Rust and push a release same-day. That level of human responsiveness creates fanatical brand loyalty.

## 3. The "Product-Led Growth" (PLG) Wedge
We need developers to use ORA at home before they force their CTOs to buy it at work.
*   **Strategy:** The core `ora-rust` binary is 100% free and open-source. Anyone can spin it up locally for Claude Desktop. 
*   **The $10M Upsell:** When a developer proposes moving their local ORA server to AWS to handle 10,000 corporate users, they'll hit the complexity ceiling of managing the Cryptographic Vault. That's when we reach out to the human CTO with the Enterprise Cloud offering.

## 4. Influencer Relations & "Founder-Led" Content
*   **Strategy:** Do not buy ads. Sponsor the top 5 highly technical Rust/AI YouTube channels (e.g., Primeagen, Theo - t3.gg equivalents in 2026). Let them try to break ORA's prompt injection shields live on stream.
*   **Founder Reality:** The CEO/Founders of ORA must post raw, unfiltered thoughts on LinkedIn and Twitter about the state of AI security. No PR agency polish. Just real, human opinions on why the industry is failing at security and how ORA fixes it.

## 5. RevOps: Unifying the Human Experience
*   **Strategy:** When an Enterprise Lead requests a demo, they don't get an automated webinar. They get a 1-on-1 Teams call with an ORA Security Solutions Architect.
*   **The Pitch:** "We know you're dealing with immense pressure to deploy AI safely. We built ORA so you don't have to build a security kernel from scratch. Let's look at your architecture."
