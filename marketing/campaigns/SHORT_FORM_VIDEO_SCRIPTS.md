## 1. The "Even Geniuses Make Mistakes" Hook (Runway/Sora Video Prompt)
**Asset to Create/Use:** `ora_einstein_meme.png` (Can use as a static hook or animate with an AI video tool).

**Video Generation Prompt (for Runway/Sora):**
> `Cinematic, hyper-realistic shot. A historical figure looking like Albert Einstein is frantically scribbling complex equations on a chalkboard. He steps back, looking proud. Unbeknownst to him, a massive glowing digital 'ERROR' sign flashes over a simple addition mistake in his formula. The camera pans to a glowing 'ORA' blue shield that instantly corrects the mistake. Dramatic lighting.`

**Voiceover/Text-to-Speech Script (Fast-paced, 15 seconds):**
"Claude 3.5 and DeepSeek are literal geniuses. You are basically hiring Einstein. 
But here’s the thing about geniuses... *[SFX: Record scratch]* 
Sometimes they write really stupid code or leak your API keys. 
If you blindly trust an LLM to run your backend, you’re playing with fire. 
Enter ORA. It’s a Rust-native security layer that sits *between* your AI and your data. 
It double-checks the math, blocks the prompt injections, and makes sure your genius doesn’t accidentally delete your database. 
Even geniuses need an editor. There’s an ORA for that. Link in bio."
